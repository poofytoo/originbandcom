Connecting Skiers

Origin is a wristband that utilizes radio and GPS technology to keep groups of skiers connected on the slopes.

FEATURES

Always Connected

How does it work? You and your friends sync up your Origins at the beginning of the day. Then you can then see their relative directions and distances with a quick glance at the screen. Your friends' locations even update with which way you are facing - try rotating your phone to see what it looks like.

NOTIFY WITH A PING

Want to tell your friends it's time to head in for lunch or meet to take a run together? Press the top button of the Origin to ping your friends. They press the bottom button to acknowledge your ping, and now you know they've seen it.

EMERGENCY CALL

If you fall and need help, you can send an emergency notification to your friends. Ski resorts can opt to integrate this with their emergency response program to direct help to your exact location.
